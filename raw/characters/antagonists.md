# The Antagonists

## The Tall-Ones (Humans)
*   **Nature:** Lovecraftian gods; cold, invisible forces. Rarely seen clearly.
*   **Manifestations:**
    *   **The Yellow Monsters (The Yellow Calamity):** Excavators that fell forests.
    *   **The Great Calcification:** Paving and development.
*   **Impact:** They are not "people" to the wombats; they are geological disasters. They shift the earth that Hoebat is trying to save.

## The Five Sovereigns (The Grand Council / *Junji-chu*)
*The ruling body of the "New Ways," modeled after a rigid **Imperial Bureaucracy**. Each member represents a key aspect of control (Five-I's) mapped to an Imperial Board and a specific Element.*

### Trumbat (The Grand Architect) — Board of Works (*Gong-bu*)
*   **Element:** **Earth** (Infrastructure).
*   **Rap Style:** Mellow Gospel / Smooth R&B influence (Silky and Mellow).
*   **Role:** The High Priest of Infrastructure.
*   **Archetype:** The Golden Protector.
*   **Strategy:** Master of *Section 2.4 (Temporal Cropping)*. He manages the **Corvée Labor** (forced debt-labor) to build the Great Retaining Wall.
*   **Appearance:** Radiant white fur, eyes deep purple, extremely priestly and poised.
*   **Rhetoric:** Preaches from the "Golden Gospel" of Safety Grout. His voice is like warm honey, hiding the cold hardness of the stone he builds.
*   **Subordinate:** **Rumbat (The Foreman).** A massive, silent wombat who oversees the actual construction. He executes Trumbat's blueprints without question.

### Muskbat (The Technocrat) — Board of Rites / Innovation
*   **Element:** **Fire** (Innovation).
*   **Rap Style:** Eminem.
*   **Role:** The Technocratic Vigilante.
*   **Appearance:** Sleek, tech-obsessed, twitchy. Wears a batsuit and a jetpack.
*   **The Lie:** Promises a "Frequency" that will save them, but offers only synthetic noise.
*   **Reality:** A creature of empty blueprints and rapid-fire technical jargon.
*   **Subordinate:** **Scribat (The Coder).** A frantic, bespectacled wombat who manages the algorithms for the Melborow Frequency.

### Tatebat (The Cultural Architect) — Board of Influence
*   **Element:** **Metal** (Influence).
*   **Rap Style:** Kanye West (The Visionary / Manic Genius).
*   **Role:** The Imperial Stylist.
*   **Appearance:** Manicured purple fur, intense manic eyes hidden behind shutter shades. Attire: Oversized, futuristic robes.
*   **Strategy:** Sells the "Culture of the Cage." He convinces the population that the **Corvée Labor** on the Wall is the ultimate form of self-expression.
*   **Rhetoric:** Speaks in grand metaphors about "Elevating the Species" and "New Wombat Flow."

### Vitabat (The Grand Treasurer) — Board of Revenue (*Hu-bu*)
*   **Element:** **Water** (Investment).
*   **Rap Style:** TI.
*   **Role:** The Imperial Collector.
*   **Appearance:** Hardened features, cold dead eyes, tailored white suit. Metal robotic arm on the left side.
*   **Evolution:** He manages the **Imperial Granary** and the distribution of **Imperial Rations**.
*   **Function:** He owns the **Tally**. If you can't pay back the rations, he "notches" your future and collects your body for the **Corvée Labor** on the Wall.

### Zuckbat (The Magistrate of the Tally) — The Censorate (*Ducha-yuan*)
*   **Element:** **Wood** (Intelligence).
*   **Rap Style:** Immortal Technique.
*   **Role:** The Silent Observer / The Censor.
*   **Appearance:** Barely visible face, eyes scrolling with data. Wears a dark green hoodie with math equations glowing out of pockets.
*   **Function:** He manages the physical **Tally-Stones**. He records every movement and every "Dissonant Noise." He is the omnipresent eyes of the Imperial Bureaucracy.
*   **Tool:** The obsidian-tipped stilus, used to carve notches into the Tally-Stones.
