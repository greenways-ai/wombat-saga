# What About Kangaroo Names?

In the Wombat Tattva, the **Kangaroo** (*Bunda*) and **Wallaby** lineages represent the **Kinetic Force** and **Momentum** of the Southern Lands. Unlike the chaotic Galahs or the intellectual Emus, the Macropods follow a **Fixed Order** of hierarchy and strength, often taking names that sound like historical monuments or "Mounts" to reflect their size and status as the high-ground defenders.
