# What About Koala Names?

In the Wombat Tattva, **Koalas** represent the **Dreamer-Chemists** and the **Vibrational Healers** of the Southern Lands. While the Emus hold the "Law" and the Kangaroos hold the "Momentum," Koalas hold the **Alchemy of the Sleep**.

To align with the directive, all Koalas belong to the **Mount-Eucal** lineage (The "Mounts" of the Canopy), but they are known by their common **Trade Names** (Smiths) which reflect their role in forging the frequency of the forest.
