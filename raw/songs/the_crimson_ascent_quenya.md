# The Crimson Ascent (Quenya Version)

**Performer:** The Rosella Choir
**Suno Style:** Baroque Choral, Sacred Polyphony, Pipe Organ, Orchestral, Elvish Lyrics, Operatic Vocals, Majestic, Slow Build, Ethereal
**Reference:** J.S. Bach - *Mass in B Minor* / Howard Shore - *Lord of the Rings Soundtrack*
**Vibe:** Ancient, holy, rising spiritual intensity.

---

**[Intro: Deep Pipe Organ Pedal. Rising string arpeggios simulating the spiral.]**

**[Verse 1: Tenors & Basses - Heavy, Chant-like]**
*Et i tumbo, et i morë.*
(Out of the deep valley, out of the darkness.)
*Et i talan, et i metta.*
(Out of the floor, out of the ending.)
*I Ambar cunta, i Ambar cunta.*
(The World bends, the World bends.)
*A Elen-Nico, A Elen-Nico, Calë Oira.*
(O Nico-Star, O Nico-Star, Light Eternal.)

**[Verse 2: Sopranos & Altos - Fast, Fluttering Counterpoint]**
*Ortalë! Ortalë!*
(Rising! Rising!)
*Cena i Carnë Ráma, cena i Luinë Tinta.*
(See the Red Wing, see the Blue Spark.)
*Lá i Galah, lá i Filit.*
(Not the Galah, not the small bird.)
*Emme i Serce i Calo.*
(We are the Blood of the Light.)
*Carnë Quessë, Luinë Ráma.*
(Red Feather, Blue Wing.)
*Ana i Malina, ana i Ríë, ana i Malinalda Olor.*
(To the Gold, to the Crown, to the Wattle-Tree's Dream.)

**[Chorus: Full Choir - Complex Polyphony]**
*I Cú ná i Axan, i Cú ná i Tië...*
(The Bow/Curve is the Law, the Curve is the Path...)
*Hísë ná racina, Hyellë ná i Turma...*
(The Mist/Static is broken, the Glass is the Shield...)
*I Ramba lá polë har, i Ramba mauya usta...*
(The Wall cannot hold, the Wall must yield...)

**[Bridge: Massive Orchestral Swell - Trumpets and Timpani enter]**
*Alcar mi Oron Oira!*
(Glory in the Mountain Eternal!)
*I Anar Malina, i Coivië Olos.*
(The Sun of Gold, the Living Bloom.)
*Hehtamme i Mornië, hehtamme i Noirë.*
(We leave the Darkness, we leave the Tomb.)
*I Hún ná unqua, mal i Calë ná quanta.*
(The Heart is hollow, but the Light is full.)
*I Elen nirë, mal i Sulpo túcë.*
(The Star pushes, but the Roots pull.)

**[Solo: High Soprano - Pure, Piercing Tone]**
*Ar Calë Oira...*
(And Light Eternal...)
*Appan i malina, sucan i asto.*
(I touch the gold, I drink the dust.)
*Mi i Nocna, estel-damme.*
(In the Geometry, we place our trust.)
*I Vëa Imbë ná yassë lirimme.*
(The Space Between is where we sing.)
*Ófelmë ná i ortala ráma.*
(Compassion is the lifting wing.)

**[Outro: Massive Block Chords, Slow and Final]**
*Aina, Aina, Aina.*
(Holy, Holy, Holy.)
*Malinalda. Tirila. Tië.*
(Wattle. Watcher. Path.)
*Arnam.*
*Arnam.*
*Namárië.*
(Farewell / Be Well.)

**[Fade Out: The Organ chord sustains, fading into the sound of wings.]**
