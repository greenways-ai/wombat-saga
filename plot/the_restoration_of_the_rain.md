* **The Dog (The Float)**: Once the ground is softened and the air is cleared of the Melborow Frequency, the **Dog-Brahman** can finally achieve "The Float," rising above the static into pure consciousness.

The "Rain" returns when the **Great Crystalisation** is reversed. As the "Tall-Ones" begin to dream in warmth again, the spiritual moisture of Arnam descends back into the deep earth:

* **Softening the Mud**: The soil becomes a "lush, muddy mess" again, allowing for flexible, organic tunnels that can survive the geological shifts of the human world.
