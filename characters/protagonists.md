# The Protagonists

## "The 5" (The New Primes)
*The team destined to replace the fallen Three Primes and restore balance in the era of the Jacaranda.*

### Bash (The Wallaby)
*   **Rap Style:** Future mixed with Digital Static, Mumble rap.
*   **Language:** Talks in "Skibidi." No one on the team understands him, but he functions as a universal translator, able to talk to everyone else outside the group.
*   **Role:** The Communicator / The Digital Interface.

### Merl (The Emu)
*   **Rap Style:** Common mixed with Spaghetti Western, Abstract Metaphors.
*   **Personality:** Very serious.
*   **Role:** Sword Master and Master Tactician.
*   **Skill:** Uses the **Ninth Thread** to predict battlefield movements.

### Cooroo (The Galah)
*   **Rap Style:** Matisyahu, Reggae beat.
*   **Personality:** Overly optimistic ("Everything will work out").
*   **Role:** The Wise Rabbi / Seer.
*   **Ability:** Sees far and into the future. Despite his lightness, he ends up being the wisest of the group.

### Pokero (The Dingo)
*   **Rap Style:** Twista, Aussie Crypto.
*   **Role:** The Technician / Master of the Static.
*   **Skill:** Extremely technical. He manages the **Static**â€”the excess digital waste of the God-kinds (Humans) that festers in Arnamland. He uses "Static-Logic" to turn this data-rot into a tactical advantage for Kappa Mu.
*   **Background:** Leader of the **Static-Unit** on the Westside. He views the Static not as filth, but as the raw material of the new era.

### Hoebat (The Wombat)
*   **Rap Style:** Cuban Link and (Canto).
*   **Personality:** Loyal, family-oriented, very considerate.
*   **Role:** The Heart of the Group.
*   **Origin:** Born of the **Dust-Bearers** (Old Ways), he bridges the gap between the ancient earth and the new fight.

## The Wombokis (The Archetypes)
*Figures encountered by Hoebat during his wanderings that shaped his worldview.*

### The Fortune Teller
*   **Species:** A blind Bandicoot.
*   **Prophecy:** "You will seek a hole, but find a mountain."

### The Merchant
*   **Species:** A greedy Echidna.
*   **Activity:** Selling "pre-dug luxury voids."
*   **Significance:** The first sign of the "High-Nose corruption."

### Uncle Hangbat (The Beggar Fool)
*   **Role:** The Long-Lost Uncle.
*   **Disguise:** Posed as a "Beggar Fool" on the street.
*   **Secret:** Feigned madness to escape the "Fixed Borow" of the family.
*   **Action:** When Hoebat gave him a fermented root, he whispered the secret frequency of the **Dingo Code**.
