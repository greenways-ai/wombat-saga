# Characters of The Wombat Worlds Saga

This document outlines the key figures in the Wombat Saga, categorizing them by their role in the conflict between the "Old Ways" and the "New Ways" of Melborow.

## The Protagonists: "The 5"
*The new team rising to replace the fallen Primes.*

*   **Bash (Wallaby):** The Communicator (Skibidi/Future rap).
*   **Merl (Emu):** The Tactician (Common/Western rap).
*   **Cooroo (Galah):** The Seer (Reggae/Matisyahu rap).
*   **Pokero (Dingo):** The Tech / Master of the Static (Twista/Crypto rap).
*   **Hoebat (Wombat):** The Heart (Cuban Link/Canto rap).

*See [[characters/protagonists|The Protagonists]] for full profiles.*

## The Wombokis (The Archetypes)
*Figures encountered by Hoebat during his wanderings that shaped his worldview.*

### The Fortune Teller
*   **Species:** A blind Bandicoot.
*   **Prophecy:** "You will seek a hole, but find a mountain."

### The Merchant
*   **Species:** A greedy Echidna.
*   **Activity:** Selling "pre-dug luxury voids."
*   **Significance:** The first sign of the "High-Nose corruption."

### Uncle Hangbat (The Beggar Fool)
*   **Role:** The Long-Lost Uncle.
*   **Disguise:** Posed as a "Beggar Fool" on the street.
*   **Secret:** Feigned madness to escape the "Fixed Borow" of the family.
*   **Action:** When Hoebat gave him a fermented root, he whispered the secret frequency of the **Dingo Code**.
